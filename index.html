Biryani

Types

  • Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani:

    Fragrant basmati rice cooked with succulent chicken, aromatic spices, saffron, and caramelized onions

  • Hyderabadi Mutton Biryani:

    Tender mutton pieces marinated and layered with spiced rice, cooked to perfection.

  • Dum Pukht Biryani:

    A slow-cooked biryani where meat and rice are sealed together to trap the flavors and steam, resulting in a rich and aromatic dish.

  • Kacchi Biryani:

    A special style of biryani where marinated raw meat is layered with partially cooked rice and slow-cooked for a unique taste.

  • Chicken 65 Biryani:

    A fusion biryani featuring the spicy and flavorful Chicken 65 as a topping over the biryani.

  • Paneer Biryani:

    • Fragrant basmati rice cooked with marinated paneer, spices, and caramelized onions. Fish Biryani: Biryani made with marinated fish pieces, aromatic spices, and basmati rice.
